  • How do I join a sponsor organization?

    You can apply to any organization on the apply page! You will
    be notified when they make their decision regarding your application.

  • Can I join more than one sponsor?

    You can join as many sponsors as you like. However, they must accept your application to join.

  • How do I earn points?

    You earn points for good driving. When you drive well, your sponsor will
    reward you with points that can be redeemed for rewards on your home page!

  • How does my sponsor decide when I get points?

    It depends on what your sponsor deems as good driving. When you receive points,
    you will see the reason as to why you were rewarded points if you click on the 'expand' button.

  • Why are points worth different amounts for different sponsors?

    The amount of points worth a dollar on your sponsor catalog change for each
    sponsor. Some sponsors might make points worth more, or less.
